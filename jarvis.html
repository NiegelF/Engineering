AI ESP32 Assistant Description

I created an AI Assistant inspired by the likes of Jarvis from Iron Man. Fueled by two ESP32s, a microphone, and a speaker, this project elevates the concept of home automation and voice interaction. Picture this: I speak into the microphone, and the AI Assistant springs into action. The process unfolds intricately. Voice input is captured and converted into file streams, which are then whisked away to a Speech-to-Text server via the Google Cloud API. The server, akin to Jarvis deciphering Tony Stark's commands, processes the input and returns the result through a post request. This payload makes its way to the ChatGPT API, where the AI Assistant channels its inner Jarvis to generate contextually rich responses. But the magic doesn't stop there. The post request, now brimming with conversational finesse, is relayed to a Text-to-Speech serverâ€”again, courtesy of the Google Cloud API. The AI Assistant, inspired by Jarvis's eloquence, breathes life into its responses, playing them back through the speaker. In essence, this project is a homage to the seamless interaction depicted in Iron Man, where technology becomes an extension of the user. From voice input to intelligent responses, it encapsulates the spirit of Jarvis in a real-world AI Assistant, bridging the gap between fiction and innovation.
