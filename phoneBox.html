Phone Lock Box Description

I created a smart lock box designed to curb screen time using an Arduino Nano, servo motor, LCD display, three buttons, and a precision linear gear system as the locking mechanism. The concept is simple yet effective—I wanted a solution to reduce phone usage, so I developed a box where you can stash your phone. Here's the drill: the first button lets you increase the lock time in 30-minute increments, and the second one does the opposite, decreasing the time. Once you've set your preferred duration, hit the third button—the start button—and watch as the box locks up, phone securely tucked inside. It's like a digital detox in a box! And here's the kicker—it stays locked until the timer runs its course, ensuring a break from the screen until the designated time is up. A creative blend of technology and self-discipline, this lock box stands as a tangible solution to the digital age's screen time conundrum.
